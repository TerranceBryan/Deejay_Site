A little story about dj viki

Dj viki, real name is Victor cherogony and is a first born of 6 siblings, who started dreaming of becoming a DJ at a very young age. After completing primary school at scholar grammer academy, he joined njoro boys highschool and that's where his passion for deejaying started.

He could draw a deck and the mixer and in his classbook and start meditating as if in a live perfoming event.

At home he used (cross-Dj ~{a mobile application}) for deejaying to practice and record short mixes. After highschool Dj Viki went to system unit dj academy and studied diploma in deejaying and that's where his career as a professional DJ started.

DJ Viki is also a CEO of top beat entertainment company and a video director. Having directed about 8 songs and of them is TUKO PARTY BY Wa2ndu republik.

DJ Viki also went to Eldoret National polytechnic to study diploma in information studies, he is also a forex trader.
